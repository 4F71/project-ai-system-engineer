# Proof — Square Root Numerical Fluency

## Example 1
√361

19² = 361  
Result: 19

---

## Example 2
√0.0004

0.0004 = 4 / 10 000  
√0.0004 = 2 / 100 = 0.02

---

## Example 3
√1.21

1.21 = 121 / 100  
√1.21 = 11 / 10 = 1.1

---

## Ordering Check

0.04 < 0.16  
√0.04 = 0.2  
√0.16 = 0.4  

Since 0.04 < 0.16 → 0.2 < 0.4
